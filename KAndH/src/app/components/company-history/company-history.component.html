Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

2016

Company Founded

K And H Transport, Inc. was established in Westland, MI, with just 2 trucks and a vision to revolutionize the logistics industry. From day one, we focused on providing reliable and efficient transportation services.

2016

USDOT and MC Registration

We obtained our USDOT Number (2886840) and MC Number (969186), allowing us to operate as an interstate carrier. This marked the beginning of our journey to serve clients across the United States.

2018

Expansion of Services

We expanded our services to include general freight, refrigerated food, and household goods. Our fleet grew to meet the increasing demand for reliable transportation solutions.

2020

Safety and Compliance Milestone

K And H Transport achieved a perfect safety record with zero out-of-service violations. Our commitment to safety and compliance has been a cornerstone of our success.

2022

Industry Recognition

We were recognized as a trusted partner in the logistics industry, serving clients with over 128,000 miles driven annually. Our dedication to excellence continues to set us apart.

2024

Future Goals

We aim to expand our fleet, enhance our technology, and continue delivering exceptional service to our clients. Our vision is to become a leader in sustainable and innovative logistics solutions.

Company Overview

K And H Transport, Inc. is a trusted name in the freight and logistics industry, operating under USDOT Number 2886840 and MC Number 969186. Since our founding in 2016, we have grown into a reliable partner for businesses across the United States.

  • Total Trucks: 2
  • Total Drivers: 2
  • MCS-150 Mileage (2024): 128,001 miles
  • Carrier Operation: Interstate
  • Hazardous Material: No
  • Services: General Freight, Refrigerated Food, Household Goods
